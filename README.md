🎬 Netflix Movies & TV Shows – Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

📌 Project Overview
This project presents a comprehensive Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) of the Netflix Movies and TV Shows dataset.
The objective of this analysis is to explore Netflix’s content strategy, audience targeting, and content growth trends using Python-based data analysis and visualization techniques.

📊 Dataset Information
Dataset: Netflix Movies and TV Shows
Total Records: 8,807
Total Features: 12
Time Period Covered: 1925 – 2021
Source: Kaggle

🛠 Tools & Technologies Used
Python
Pandas
NumPy
Matplotlib
Seaborn
Plotly
WordCloud

🧹 Data Cleaning & Preprocessing
The following preprocessing steps were performed:
Checked dataset structure and summary statistics
Identified missing values
Handled missing values in director, cast, and country
Converted date_added to datetime format
Extracted year_added for trend analysis
Cleaned duration column for numerical analysis
Split and exploded genre column for detailed genre analysis

📈 Exploratory Data Analysis
The project includes the following visualizations and analyses:

1️⃣ Movies vs TV Shows
Comparison of the distribution of content types available on Netflix.

2️⃣ Content Growth Over Time
Line plots showing:
Number of releases per year
Content added per year
Movies vs TV Shows trend over time

3️⃣ Top Genres
Bar plots and pie charts highlighting the most common genres on Netflix.

4️⃣ Top Countries Producing Content
Bar charts and interactive Choropleth Map showing content distribution across countries.

5️⃣ Ratings Distribution
Analysis of content ratings such as TV-MA, TV-14, PG-13, etc.

6️⃣ Movie Duration Analysis
Histogram showing the distribution of movie durations.

7️⃣ Top Directors and Actors
Analysis of most frequently appearing directors and cast members.

8️⃣ Advanced Visualizations
Interactive World Map (Choropleth)
Treemap (Type + Genre)
Bubble Chart (Release Year vs Count)
WordCloud from descriptions

🔍 Key Insights
Movies slightly dominate over TV Shows on Netflix.
The United States produces the highest amount of content.
Content production increased significantly after 2015.
TV-MA and TV-14 are the most common ratings.
Drama, Comedy, and International content are the most popular genres.
Most movies fall within the 80–120 minute duration range.
